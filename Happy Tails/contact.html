Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the adoption process take?

The adoption process typically takes 3-5 business days. This includes the application review, home visit (if applicable), and final approval. We want to ensure every match is perfect!

Do you charge an adoption fee?

Yes, we have a modest adoption fee that covers vaccinations, spaying/neutering, microchipping, and initial medical care. The fee varies depending on the age and medical needs of the puppy. Contact us for specific pricing.

Can I volunteer at Happy Tails?

Absolutely! We always welcome volunteers. Whether you can walk dogs, help with events, foster puppies, or assist with administrative tasks, there's a role for you! Fill out the contact form above with "Volunteer Opportunities" as the subject.

What if I can't adopt but want to help?
